About Us

Lovely Event's & Entertainments Pvt. Ltd


Welcome to Lovely Event's of Mumbai, a premier event management and wedding planning company serving the vibrant cities of Delhi and Mumbai. We specialize in curating an extensive array of services catering to both corporate and personal events.

At Lovely Event's, we excel in orchestrating a diverse spectrum of events, ranging from live shows, celebrity galas, and entertainment extravaganzas to product launches, theme parties, birthdays, fashion shows, gazal nights, and more. Our seasoned event planners, boasting over 20 years of full-time experience, are committed to ensuring the splendor and success of your event.

We take pride in offering innovative and bespoke ideas, tailored to achieve 100% client satisfaction. As a comprehensive hub for event and wedding planning needs, we cover every aspect, including venue selection, hotel bookings, hospitality, decor recommendations, catering, celebrity management, and entertainment options. Our team is dedicated to transforming your envisioned event into an unforgettable reality.

Lovely Event's of Mumbai is your go-to destination for creating mesmerizing and memorable experiences. Let us turn your dreams into extraordinary events.
